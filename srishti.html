Srishti Luthra

Matcha! Chai! Coffee! Boba! Tea!

From matcha to chai to lattes to tea, I love to have a soothing drink by my side at all times.

The Specific Drinks I love

  • Iced Lattes, usually in the morning
  • Bubble Tea, usually milk tea, although I'll make an exception for lychee fruit tea
  • Hot Tea, specifically spearmint, to wind down at night
  • Iced Matcha, as an afternoon pick-me-up
  • A Hot cup of chai, the most soothing drink one could ask for
